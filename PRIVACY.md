# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 29 April 2026_

This Privacy Policy describes how **The Odyssey** (the "App", package ID
`com.harryhh.theodyssey`) handles information.

## Summary

**The Odyssey does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.** It is
a single-player offline narrative game. Your save data lives on your device
and nowhere else.

## Information stored on your device

The App stores your in-progress game (current location, stats, narrative log,
and which figures you have encountered) on your device using Android's
standard application storage. This data:

- Is stored only on your device.
- Is not transmitted to us or to any third party.
- Can be deleted at any time by clearing the App's data in Android Settings,
  or by uninstalling the App.

## Information we collect

**None.** The App contains:

- No analytics or telemetry.
- No advertising or ad SDKs.
- No user accounts or sign-in.
- No microphone, camera, contacts, or location access.
- No social-network integrations.

## Network traffic

The App is fully playable offline. The only network traffic the App may
generate is a check for over-the-air content updates from
[Expo Application Services (EAS)](https://expo.dev), the platform used to
deliver updates. When this check occurs, EAS may receive standard request
metadata (such as your IP address and basic device/app version information)
solely to determine which update, if any, applies to your install. We do not
receive or store this metadata. Expo's handling of such data is governed by
the [Expo Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy).

## Children's privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any
data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Permissions

The App requests no runtime permissions on Android beyond what is required
to install and update itself.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted in this repository
with a new "Last updated" date.
